ABOUT US

Established since 1992 and has become on of Glasgow's most popular and well known Indian, Chinese and Italian cuisine restaurant. Famous for its quality of food and standard of service, with a choice of the finest wines available.

The Restaurant has the perfect setting for all special occasions and is also suitable for more intimate and atmosphere dining.

Latway Catering offers an extensive range of menus to suit all budgets and tastes. As well as our Celebration and pre-theatre menus, we also have a lunch menu and our extensive Latway Menu is always available.
